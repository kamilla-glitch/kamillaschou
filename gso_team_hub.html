✅ How to Succeed Day-to-Day
📩
Introduction email sent by AE/AM upon project assignment
Once you've been assigned to a project, the AE/AM will send the intro email. Change the DSR status from Assigned → Seller Outreach Complete when you see the email in your inbox.
🔁
3×3 Outreach Rule — then close
Attempt contact 3 times, every 3 days. On the 4th email, notify seller of training request cancellation. If KOC is not booked within 12 days of intro (intro + 3×3 days reminders), mark DSR as LOST and communicate to AE.

⚠️ Ensure you let the AE know every time you send a follow-up email to the seller — either by tagging them in Chatter or leaving a comment in the deal Slack channel.
📅
Contact every 3 business days once in-implementation
Keep momentum and flag blockers early. If a seller is not ready to proceed, reject the DSR and tell the AE to resubmit when ready.
⚡
24-hour email response SLA (while in implementation)
Even "I'll get back to you soon" is fine — sellers must not be left in the dark. Once handed over to AE/CS/AM, refer inquiries accordingly.
📆
Book ALL training sessions during the KOC using the GANTT chart
Schedule: Dashboard Training → Menu Review (if needed) → SO Training → POS/App Training → Final Q&A. Align all dates to the seller's desired go-live date and list them in the KOC recap email.
📋 After Every Virtual Training Session — Required Steps
Complete all three steps after every virtual training session. These are expected of your role and stated in the GSO Playbook.
1
Find notes in the DSA from the completed virtual training (or closeout form from Site Survey/Installation) and add notes in Chatter and tag the AE, or post in the designated Slack channel.
2
Find the Revenue.io call and run through your preferred AI bot to create post-call action items + post-call email. Add action items in Chatter and/or Slack and send a follow-up email to your seller. ⚠️ You must send an email to the seller after EACH touchpoint with follow-up and check-in.
3
Update the DSR on the same day with: Next Steps Next Step Date Status Seller Desired Go Live Date (every time it changes).
📖 All of the above is stated in the GSO Playbook and is expected of your role.
📝
After every seller interaction: update the DSR
Update: Next Steps · Next Step Date · Status · Seller Desired Go Live Date (every time it changes). Do this on the same day.
💬
Chatter AE/AM after every call
Tag AE with: call summary, go-live date, action items. Flag any missing hardware, incompatible printers, or blockers immediately. Link the Revenue.io recording in Chatter. Use AI tools to draft quickly.
🎯
Always have a next step booked — no exceptions
If no next step can be booked, explain why in Chatter and set a reminder date. This is non-negotiable for clean pipeline management.
⏱️
Log a DSA for every seller interaction
Every training call, task, and milestone must be logged in a Deal Support Activity on the same day it happens. Every call = a DSA.

🔧 Workflows
1
Introduction email sent by AE/AM upon project assignment
Once you've been assigned to a project, the AE/AM will send the intro email. Change the DSR status from Assigned → Seller Outreach Complete when you see the email in your inbox.
2
Pre-KOC: Account analysis
Review merchant profile & menu, check hardware (new vs. reused), ID compatibility issues, start building implementation timeline. Submit menu to BPO ASAP if possible.
3
Kickoff Call (30 mins)
Confirm hardware, network reliability, service style, go-live date. Book ALL future sessions via the GANTT chart. Cover: Dashboard → Menu Review → SO → POS/App → Q&A. Camera always on. All virtual trainings are completed by 3P — except the final Q&A, which is completed by you.
4
Post-KOC (same day)
Send KOC recap email with all booked sessions and action items. Submit tasks to BPO if you haven't done this before the kick off call (preferred for all projects). Create DSA. Update DSR status → Kick Off Call Complete. Chatter AE with go-live date and blockers.
5
Active training phase
QA BPO work before each call. Dashboard Training (90min) → Menu Review (45min, optional) → SO Training (60min) → POS/App Training (60min). Create DSA after each. Update DSR status after each session.
6
Go-Live → Handoff
Send go-live congratulations email (TextBlaze /goodbye), cc AE/AM. Q&A must be completed within 7 days of last training. After 1-week support window: update to Implementation Complete and send final email.

Onsite = Premium package process + 3P dispatch for installation and POS training. Follow all Premium steps PLUS the 3P steps below.
1
During KOC — gather onsite info
Ask site survey questions (location, network type, mounting needs, current hardware). Pre-book Q&A shortly after installation so you can iron out flow/menu changes. Book during KOC.
2
Post-KOC — create 3P DSAs via PRM
Create DSA for Site Survey + Installation + Staff Training (separate DSAs). Use the GANTT chart to book training with Tech Pantry/OWR. Include DSA number in the GANTT booking.
3
Submit BOTH: Site Survey request AND Installation request
Site survey can happen anytime after KOC. Installation must be at least 1 week after the survey (to address action items). Complete all remote trainings and config BEFORE install date.
4
Create 3P staff/team member profile on seller account
Allows partner to review account in advance, reducing dependency on someone with credentials being onsite. Delete the 3P login after install is complete.
5
Post-install wrap-up
Review closeout form in #3p-closeout-forms_apac. Handle action items (missing hardware, networking). Upload PDF to DSR. Update DSAs to Appointment Completed. Delete vendor login. Mark DSR Implementation Complete after 1 week live/trained.
🔩 Mounting rules
✅ Can mount: Drywall, FRP (notify tech in advance to bring correct tools)
❌ Cannot mount: Stainless steel, tile, concrete, brick — seller must hire contractor or use standing mounts

PRM Process — New Standard
1
KO Call — create DSA in advance
Before the KO call, create the DSA under the DSR. During KO call, use the GANTT chart to book training based on seller's preferred dates. Include the DSA number in the GANTT booking. Note: soon all DSAs will be auto-created when a DSR is created.
2
Create DSAs (Installation + Staff Training = 2 separate DSAs)
Record Type: 3rd Party Vendor · Partner: Tech Pantry or OWR · Activity Type: Site Survey / Installation / Dashboard Training etc. · Set DSA Owner to partner queue ("Queues" → partner name).
3
DSA Submission → Acceptance / Rejection
Accepted: you'll get a Salesforce Chatter notification. Rejected: Slack notification in partner's dispatch-review channel. Coordinate via Slack on counter dates. To re-submit: update Preferred Time in Scheduler tab + change DSA Owner back to partner queue.
4
Coordination timeline (critical)
Site survey: ASAP after KOC. Installation: at least 1 week after survey. Remote trainings + full account config must be complete before installation day.
5
Post-install: closeout forms & wrap-up
Check #3p-closeout-forms_apac for survey results. Handle action items. Upload PDF to DSR. Delete vendor login. Update DSAs to Appointment Completed. DSR → Implementation Complete after 1 week live/trained.

📋 DSR Status Guide
Each status reflects a specific stage in the seller's onboarding journey. Update the DSR status on the same day as each milestone — do not let it fall behind.
📈 Active Statuses — In Order Move through these in order · update on the same day as each milestone
Assigned
The DSR has been assigned to you. No action needed on the status yet — wait for the AE/AM intro email before moving forward.
Seller Outreach Complete
The intro email from AE/AM has been sent to the seller. Update to this status as soon as you see the email in your inbox.
Kick Off Call Complete
The Kickoff Call has been completed, a desired Go Live Date has been set, and there is full alignment on next steps.
Task in Progress
A task has been submitted to BPO and is being worked on — e.g. menu build, migration, or MILO.
Dashboard Training Complete
The seller has completed their Dashboard Training session with the 3P partner.
App Training Complete
The seller has completed their POS / App Training session with the 3P partner.
SO Training Complete
The seller has completed their Square Online (SO) Training session with the 3P partner.
Training Complete — Post Implementation
All purchased seller training has been completed. This begins their one-week post-support period for an optional 1-hour Q&A / post go-live session.
⚠️ Hold, Lost & Rejected Use when a seller is paused, disengaged, or the DSR can no longer proceed
On Hold
If the seller's training is postponed (construction delays, target date more than 3 weeks out, etc.) move the DSR to On Hold until the seller is ready to resume. Do not place a seller On Hold if you are actively working with them. The main reason to use On Hold is if your next step is not scheduled or unknown.
Sub-statuses for On Hold — use the closest fit:
Product Blocker Seller Non-Responsive Seller Pushed Go-Live Date Site/Premise Work Needed Waiting on AE
Lost — No Seller Contact
No response after multiple outreach attempts and 30 days in On Hold status. If you trained the seller, place in Implementation Complete instead. Do not mark Lost if you completed training.
Lost — Churn
The seller has disengaged or abandoned the process. Do not mark the seller live if you completed any training for them.
Rejected
The DSR is rejected due to incorrect escalation, lack of seller engagement, incorrect information, or the seller is not looking to kick off in the next 2–4 weeks. Use the proper sub-status when rejecting — use your best judgement for the closest fit.
✅ Implementation Complete
Once the one-week post-implementation support ends, the Onboarding Specialist sends the "Final Email" (cc'ing AE/AM). This status applies to both Active and any closed-out Hold/Lost scenarios where training was completed.
